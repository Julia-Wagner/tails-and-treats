Game Rules

To start the game, click on Start new game in the menu. Then you will be able to choose a dog character and difficulty.

Once you chose your character and difficulty the game begins. You will move your dog around the maze collecting treats. The goal of the game is to collect as many treats as possible, to make your dog happy. When you exit the maze, you will see how well you filled made the dog´s bowl.

Navigate the maze using your keyboard controls or the buttons provided underneath the maze.

Each time you start a new game, the maze is created randomly so you will face a new challenge each time you play.
